YENSA Lunar New Year

BRANDING / MERCHANDISE / PR BOX / MENUS

In 2023, YENSA began an initiative to shift more marketing focus onto Lunar New Year in celebration of the beauty brand's Asian heritage and philosophy. To ring in the Year of the Dragon, I developed the campaign's branding and designed the PR box and its contents, including a custom dragon tin and lip products with limited-edition red caps. I also designed menus and signage for three Lunar New Year influencer events held in New York, London, and Los Angeles.

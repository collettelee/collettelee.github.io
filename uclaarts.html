UCLA Summer Institute Playing Cards

BRANDING / DECK OF CARDS / MERCHANDISE

In my role as the 2023 Designer in Residence at the UCLA School of the Arts and Architecture, I designed a special edition 54-card playing deck gifted to high school students attending the sold-out UCLA Arts Summer Institute program. I developed and illustrated a summery, playful, and colorful visual style that appeals to teenage consumers, along with face cards that stray from gender norms. The designs utilize simple, “imperfect” cut-out shapes to emulate scrapbooks one would make at summer camp.

Hand-coded with <3 by Collette Lee, 2024

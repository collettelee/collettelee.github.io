Collette Lee is a California-based 2D and 3D graphic designer and aspiring art director pursuing a B.A. in Design/Media Arts at UCLA. Specializing in print and digital design, motion graphics, and 3D renderings, she has worked with world-renowned organizations such as IBM and UCLA Arts. She also dabbles in photo, video, web, and UI/UX design. She is currently seeking full-time roles in graphic design for entertainment, technology, and/or online media, where her passions lie.

In her free time, Collette enjoys improving her yoga practice, brewing her own kombucha, and trying/failing to learn how to rollerskate. Her current favorite song is "Second Night Of Summer" by BØRNS.

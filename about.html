Collette Lee is a visual designer specializing in print, motion, 3D, and UI design. She also has extensive experience in photo and video. She is an undergraduate Design/Media Arts major at UCLA, the Designer in Residence at UCLA Arts, and a former Visual Design Intern for IBM. Her passions lie in design and creative direction for both technology and entertainment media. She is a strong believer in simple yet effective design and enjoys the challenge of developing fresh and new ideas that appeal to the masses.

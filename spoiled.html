Spoiled

INTERACTIVE ANIMATION / PROJECTION / INSTALLATION

A 3D-animated interactive fridge that explores the gross, ugly side of food in a wacky way. Users are invited to hover their right hand over the real-scale projection to "open" the fridge and trigger the deterioration of its contents. This work was exhibited at the UCLA DMA 2023 Senior Capstone Show..

Hand-coded with <3 by Collette Lee, 2024

Spoiled

INTERACTIVE ANIMATION

A 3D-animated interactive fridge exploring the modern relationship between overconsumption and decay. The project evokes the liminal space of a fridge lighting up a dark kitchen at night. Users are invited to hover their right hand over the real-scale projection to "open" the fridge and trigger the deterioration of its contents. Exhibited at the UCLA DMA 2023 Senior Capstone Show.

Designed & coded by Collette

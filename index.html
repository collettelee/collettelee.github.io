Dance of Jellyfish

Concept, 3D render, and pamphlet for an art installation exhibiting latent space videos of artifically generated jellyfish. The moving images, trained on a self-curated database of jellyfish, are projected onto billowing sheets of sheer fabric to mimic the translucency and fluidity of the sea creature's form. Below sits a pool of water with a rock pathway weaving through the curtains. Light, water, texture, and machine learning technology come together to simulate an ocean swim among the jellyfish.

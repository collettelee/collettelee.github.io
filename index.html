Egg Lamp

A lamp product meant to look like a beautiful mistake, no matter what surface it is placed on. This piece of home decor is an oversized, accidentally cracked raw egg containing a glowing LED light in its yolk to illuminate any space. Through its clear egg white, environmental texture becomes a part of the work itself. Working prototype was made with laser-cut acrylic material and half of a ball pit ball. Selected for the 2023 DMA Undergraduate Exhibition at UCLA.

Dance of Jellyfish

Concept, animated 3D render, and pamphlet for an art installation exhibiting latent space videos of artifically generated jellyfish. The moving images, trained on a curated database of jellyfish, are projected onto billowing sheets of sheer fabric to mimic the translucency and fluidity of the sea creature's form. Below sits a pool of water with a rock pathway weaving through the curtains. Light, water, texture, and machine learning come together to simulate an ocean swim among jellyfish. Inspired by and created under the guidance of new media artist Refik Anadol.

Tomino's Hell

Augmented reality poster experimenting with typography, 3D animation, and sound design. Based on the cursed Japanese poem Tomino's Hell and designed in collaboration with Kathleen Yang and Sabrina Chang. Tomino's Hell is a notorious Japanese urban legend about a boy named Tomino and his journey through hell. It is said that reading the poem out loud will result in death. The animated glass beads, winding blood stream, and foggy forest background are all references to lines in the poem. The Japanese texts are phrases from the poem. The dashed boxes around certain phrases are a visual reference to Genkouyoushi paper, which are usually used by Japanese students to practice writing Kanji.

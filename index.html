Dance of Jellyfish

Concept, animated 3D render, and pamphlet for an art installation exhibiting latent space videos of artifically generated jellyfish. The moving images, trained on a curated database of jellyfish, are projected onto billowing sheets of sheer fabric to mimic the translucency and fluidity of the sea creature's form. Below sits a pool of water with a rock pathway weaving through the curtains. Light, water, texture, and machine learning come together to simulate an ocean swim among jellyfish. Inspired by and created under the guidance of new media artist Refik Anadol.

Motion, 3D, AI, Editorial, Identity

IBM AI Governance

Animated video detailing the AI governance capabilities of IBM Cloud Pak for Data. This video is intended for onboarding new team members and educating potential clients. The project combines 2D and 3D animation to convey important information about the product in a fresh and exciting way, while staying true to IBM's established visual identity. Designed, illustrated, and animated this project as a Visual Design Intern (Data and AI) for IBM Cloud and Cognitive Software.

Script by Thomas Hollifield, voice over by Nicole Jones.

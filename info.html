Collette Lee is a multidisciplinary designer and creative based in Los Angeles, CA. Currently, she works as a Brand Designer in the beauty industry and holds a B.A. in Design | Media Arts from the University of California, Los Angeles. She is passionate about fusing simplicity and play to craft compelling visual narratives. Her past clients include IBM and the UCLA School of the Arts and Architecture.

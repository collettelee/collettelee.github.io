Collette Lee is a California-based 2D and 3D graphic designer and aspiring art director completing her B.A. in Design/Media Arts at UCLA. Specializing in print and digital design, motion graphics, and 3D renderings, she has worked with world-renowned organizations such as IBM and UCLA Arts. She also dabbles in photo, video, web, and UI/UX design. She is currently seeking full-time roles in visual design for entertainment, publishing, and/or technology, where her passions lie.

In her free time, Collette enjoys improving her yoga practice and testing out new recipes in the kitchen. Her current favorite song is "Princess Diana (with Nicki Minaj)" by Ice Spice.
